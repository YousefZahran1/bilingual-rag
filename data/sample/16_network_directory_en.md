# Network Provider Directory Rules — English

## In-network vs out-of-network
Network providers bill us directly and cannot charge the member more than the agreed rate — this is called "zero balance billing." Out-of-network providers may charge above what we reimburse; the member is responsible for the difference.

## Usual and customary rate
For out-of-network claims, reimbursement is capped at the "usual and customary" rate — the average rate network providers in the same city charge for the same procedure. Any amount the out-of-network provider bills above that rate is the member's responsibility.

## Directory updates
The network provider directory is updated monthly on the member portal. Providers can join or leave the network between updates; members should confirm network status before a non-emergency visit.

## Network exception requests
If no in-network specialist for a required specialty exists within 50 km of the member's registered address, the member may request a network exception, which allows treatment at an out-of-network specialist at in-network cost-sharing rates. Requests are submitted through the member portal and decided within 5 business days.
