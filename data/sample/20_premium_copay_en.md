# Premium & Billing Schedule — English

## Annual premium
- Employee (principal member): SAR 3,200 per year, paid by the employer.
- Each elected dependent: SAR 1,800 per year, deducted from the employee's payroll in monthly installments if elected.

## Billing cycle
Dependent premiums are billed monthly, on the 1st of each month, as payroll deductions. Employers may instead pay dependent premiums in a single annual installment by written request to HR before the policy year starts.

## Grace period
If a monthly dependent premium payment is missed, coverage continues under a 15-day grace period. If payment is not received within the grace period, the dependent's coverage lapses and must be re-enrolled at the next open enrollment window, subject to new waiting periods.

## Refunds
Premiums are non-refundable once a coverage month has started, except in cases of employment termination, where the unused portion of that month's premium is refunded pro-rata.
