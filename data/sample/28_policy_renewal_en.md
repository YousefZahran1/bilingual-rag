# Policy Renewal & Waiting Periods — English

## Annual renewal
The policy renews automatically each year unless the employer cancels in writing at least 30 days before the renewal date. Members do not need to take any action for their individual coverage to continue.

## Waiting periods on new enrollment
- General coverage: 30 days from the enrollment approval date.
- Maternity benefits: 12-month waiting period before delivery is covered.
- Pre-existing conditions: 6-month exclusion period, as described in the eligibility terms.

## Portability — waiving the waiting period
If a new member had continuous coverage under a previous employer's health plan with no gap longer than 30 days, the waiting periods above are waived. Proof of prior continuous coverage (a certificate of coverage from the previous insurer) must be submitted at enrollment.

## Mid-year plan changes
Employers may not change plan tiers mid-year; plan tier changes take effect only at the next annual renewal.
