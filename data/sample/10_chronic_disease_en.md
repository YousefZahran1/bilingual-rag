# Chronic Disease Management Program — English

## Eligibility
Members with a confirmed diagnosis of diabetes, hypertension, or both may enroll in the Chronic Disease Management Program at no extra cost. Enrollment is voluntary and can be requested through the member portal or by calling the care management line.

## Program benefits
- Quarterly nurse check-in calls to review medication adherence and symptoms.
- HbA1c testing: covered twice per year for enrolled diabetic members.
- Home blood pressure monitors: one device every 2 years, covered 100%, for enrolled hypertensive members.
- Insulin pumps: covered up to SAR 8,000, replaceable every 3 years, pre-authorization required.
- Dietitian consultations: 4 per year, covered 100%.

## Medication adherence
Members enrolled in the program receive a 90-day supply of maintenance medications per pharmacy visit instead of the standard 30-day supply, to reduce refill friction.

## Exiting the program
Members may withdraw from the program at any time without affecting their base plan coverage.
