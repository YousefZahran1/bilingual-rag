# International & Travel Emergency Coverage — English

## Coverage outside Saudi Arabia
Emergency medical care received outside the Kingdom is covered up to SAR 200,000 per trip, for trips of 90 days or less. Elective or planned treatment sought abroad is not covered under this benefit.

## Notification requirement
Members must notify the 24/7 assistance hotline within 48 hours of receiving emergency treatment abroad. Claims for overseas emergency treatment submitted without this notification may be denied.

## Repatriation
If a treating physician abroad certifies that the member is stable enough to travel but requires ongoing care, medically necessary repatriation to a hospital in Saudi Arabia is covered, arranged through the 24/7 assistance hotline.

## Trips longer than 90 days
Members traveling abroad for more than 90 days (for example on secondment or study leave) are not covered under this benefit and should arrange separate travel insurance for the portion of the trip beyond day 90.
