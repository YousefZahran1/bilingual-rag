# Dental & Optical Rider — English

## Dental
- Routine cleanings: 2 per calendar year, covered 100%.
- Fillings and simple restorations: covered at 80%.
- Root canal treatment: covered at 70%, pre-authorization required above SAR 2,000.
- Orthodontics (braces): not covered for members 18 and over. Covered at 50% for dependents under 18.
- Cosmetic dental procedures (whitening, veneers): excluded.

## Optical
- Comprehensive eye exam: 1 per calendar year, covered 100%.
- Frames and prescription lenses: annual allowance of SAR 500.
- Contact lenses: annual allowance of SAR 300, in place of (not in addition to) the frames/lenses allowance.
- Laser vision correction (LASIK): excluded.

## How to claim
Dental and optical claims follow the same submission process as medical claims — see the Claims Submission Process document. Keep itemized receipts; the optical allowance is reimbursed against the receipt total, up to the annual cap.
