# Telemedicine Coverage — English

## Virtual GP consultations
Virtual consultations with a general practitioner are covered 100%, unlimited visits, available 24/7 through the member app. No copay applies.

## Virtual specialist consultations
Virtual specialist consultations are covered at the same 20% copay as in-person specialist visits.

## E-prescriptions
Prescriptions issued during a telemedicine consultation are valid at any network pharmacy, in-person or via the pharmacy delivery service.

## What telemedicine does not cover
Telemedicine cannot be used for services that require physical examination or equipment — physical therapy, imaging, lab draws, and procedures all require an in-person visit. Teletherapy for mental health is covered separately under the mental health benefit.

## Technical requirements
The member app requires a stable internet connection; consultations can be conducted in Arabic or English based on member preference at booking.
