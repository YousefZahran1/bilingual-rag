# Claims Submission Process — English

## Network providers
When you visit a network provider, the provider files the claim directly with us. You don't need to do anything beyond presenting your insurance card.

## Out-of-network providers
1. Pay the provider out of pocket.
2. Within 90 days of the service date, submit a reimbursement request via the member portal: https://members.example.sa
3. Required documents: original itemized invoice, prescription if applicable, claim form (downloadable from portal), copy of national ID.
4. Reimbursement is processed within 21 business days for complete claims.
5. Incomplete claims will be returned with a request for missing information.

## Appeals
If a claim is denied, you have 60 days to appeal in writing to claims-appeals@example.sa with supporting documentation.
