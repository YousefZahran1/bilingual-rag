# Claims Appeal & Grievance Procedure — English

## Two-tier appeal process
If a claim is denied, members have a two-tier appeal process.

**Tier 1 — Internal review.** Submit a written appeal with supporting documentation within 60 days of the denial. Internal review is completed within 15 business days.

**Tier 2 — External review.** If unsatisfied with the Tier 1 decision, members may escalate to the CCHI ombudsman for an independent external review. The Tier 2 request must be filed within 30 days of the Tier 1 decision.

## Grievances (non-claim complaints)
Complaints not related to a specific claim denial — for example service quality, wait times, or provider conduct — are handled as grievances rather than appeals. Grievances are submitted through the member portal and resolved within 10 business days.

## What to include in an appeal
- The original claim reference number.
- A written explanation of why the member disagrees with the denial.
- Any additional supporting medical documentation not already submitted.
