# Pre-authorization Requirements — English

## Procedures requiring pre-authorization
- Elective (non-emergency) inpatient admission.
- MRI and CT scans costing more than SAR 2,000.
- Specialty medications (see the pharmacy formulary).
- Physiotherapy sessions beyond the first 6 sessions per condition.
- Dental surgery, including impacted wisdom tooth extraction.
- Bariatric (weight-loss) surgery.

## How to request pre-authorization
Your treating physician submits the pre-authorization request through the provider portal, attaching clinical justification. Members do not need to submit pre-authorization requests themselves at network providers.

## Turnaround time
- Standard requests: decided within 5 business days.
- Urgent requests (physician-flagged): decided within 24 hours.

## Penalty for skipping pre-authorization
If a procedure that required pre-authorization is performed without it, the claim is still processed but reimbursement is reduced by a 30% penalty, except in genuine emergencies where pre-authorization was not medically possible in advance.
