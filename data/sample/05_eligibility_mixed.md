# Eligibility & Member Onboarding (mixed AR/EN)

New members must complete enrollment within 30 days of their hire date / تاريخ التوظيف.
Required documents:
- صورة الهوية الوطنية أو الإقامة (national ID or Iqama copy)
- Employer letter / خطاب صاحب العمل
- Family members (if any) / أفراد العائلة (إن وجدوا): national ID per dependent

Coverage start date: the day after enrollment is approved. The member receives an SMS in Arabic and an email in English with the digital insurance card.

For pre-existing conditions, a 6-month exclusion may apply unless the previous employer's plan was continuous. للحالات المرضية السابقة، قد يتم تطبيق استثناء 6 أشهر ما لم تكن خطة صاحب العمل السابقة متصلة.
