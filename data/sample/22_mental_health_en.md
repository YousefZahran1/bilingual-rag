# Mental Health & Wellness Coverage — English

## Outpatient mental health care
Outpatient psychiatry and psychology sessions are covered at 80%, up to 12 sessions per calendar year. Sessions beyond 12 require pre-authorization based on clinical necessity.

## Inpatient psychiatric care
Inpatient psychiatric admission is covered up to 14 days per year. Pre-authorization is required except in emergency admissions, where notification within 48 hours is sufficient.

## Teletherapy
Teletherapy (virtual counseling) sessions are included under the telemedicine benefit and are counted against the same 12-session outpatient limit as in-person sessions.

## Wellness reimbursement
Members may claim an annual wellness reimbursement of SAR 500 toward a gym membership or fitness program, submitted once per calendar year with proof of payment.

## Exclusions
Couples counseling not related to a diagnosed mental health condition, and executive coaching, are not covered.
