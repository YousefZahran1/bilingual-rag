# Emergency & Ambulance Services — English

## Emergency room visits
- Emergency room copay: SAR 100 per visit.
- The SAR 100 copay is waived if the member is admitted to the hospital from the emergency room.
- Emergency care is covered at any hospital, including out-of-network, when the condition is life-threatening.

## Ground ambulance
Ground ambulance transport within the Kingdom is covered at 100% with no copay, for genuine emergencies.

## Air ambulance
Air ambulance transport is covered up to SAR 50,000 for life-threatening emergencies where ground transport is not medically appropriate. Pre-notification to the 24/7 assistance line is required within 24 hours of transport, or as soon as reasonably possible if the member was incapacitated.

## What counts as an emergency
A medical emergency is a condition of sudden onset that a reasonable person would believe requires immediate treatment to avoid serious harm — for example chest pain, severe bleeding, loss of consciousness, or major trauma. Non-urgent conditions treated in an emergency room are subject to standard outpatient copay instead of the emergency copay.
