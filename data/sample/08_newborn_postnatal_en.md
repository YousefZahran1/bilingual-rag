# Newborn & Postnatal Care — English

## Newborn well-baby visits
- 5 well-baby checkups covered in the first year of life, at 0% copay.
- Routine vaccinations per the Ministry of Health schedule: covered 100%.
- Newborn hearing and metabolic screening: covered 100%, must be completed within 2 weeks of birth.

## NICU and newborn complications
- Neonatal intensive care (NICU): covered up to a lifetime cap of SAR 100,000 per newborn.
- Congenital condition treatment in the first 90 days of life: covered under the same terms as the mother's maternity benefit, no separate waiting period.

## Postnatal maternal care
- One postnatal maternal checkup within 6 weeks of delivery: covered 100%.
- Postnatal complications requiring readmission within 30 days of delivery: covered under inpatient benefits, no additional waiting period.

## Circumcision
Circumcision for male newborns is covered when performed by a network provider within the first year of life.
