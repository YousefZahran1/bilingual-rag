# Saudi Health Plan Coverage — Plan A

## Outpatient
- Consultations with primary care physicians: covered at network providers, no copay.
- Specialist consultations: 20% copay outside the referral network.
- Diagnostics (lab + imaging): covered when ordered by a network physician.

## Inpatient
- Hospital admissions: pre-authorization required for elective procedures.
- Daily room rate cap: SAR 1,500/day for shared rooms.
- ICU: covered up to SAR 5,000/day.

## Maternity
- Antenatal care: 6 visits covered.
- Delivery: covered for normal and emergency C-section after a 12-month waiting period.

## Pharmacy
- Generic medications: 100% covered when prescribed.
- Brand-name medications: 50% covered.
- Specialty medications require pre-authorization.

## Exclusions
- Cosmetic surgery, fertility treatment, alternative medicine.
